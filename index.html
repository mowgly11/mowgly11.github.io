My skills include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS and various frameworks and libraries. I have experience in both Front-End and Back-End development, which allows me to build dynamic and responsive websites that provide a seamless user experience.

In addition to my technical skills, I also have experience working with Adobe Photoshop. I am always striving to improve my skills and stay current with the latest technologies in order to provide the best possible solutions for my clients. I am confident that my technical skills and graphic design experience make me a valuable asset for any project. I am a quick learner and a team player, I have a keen attention to detail, and I am able to meet tight deadlines.
